Be Brave ! Even if you're not, Pretend to be.

25 July 2020

For me, the architect is about aesthetics and construction building. People like to ask what you have to prepare before getting into an architecture field. I can say creativity. Most people say you need to be brave and smart to become an architect. But for me, we can't stop ourselves from doing something that we love just because we are not good at it. You can learn along the way while you are studying. Just like me, I am not a good student, and I still can do it. What you have to prepare is your patient and good at handling criticism. I was born to an architect family. As I remember, my dad wanted my sister to study architecture because my sister is not the kind of patient person. After she gets into architect industry, I can see the improvement in her. Before getting into the architecture field, I never expected that it would combine math and some physic, I just know it all about design and finding the concept. In contrast, when we are working on a project as an architect, we are not only focused on the design. it's not obligatory to be good at math or science. But if you take a look at the college curriculum at several universities, you'll find that a basic knowledge of mathematics is required for most degrees and for most college major, the architecture field has always been an aggregate of art and science. Architects design and plan spaces, with consideration for looks (aesthetics), safety and accessibility, functionality for the client, cost, and specifying construction elements and processes that do not damage the ecosystem and atmosphere. Architecture is in the middle between interior design and engineering, which is the first thing we should do to start the project. The reason that I chose to study Architecture because I think it is a potential major and the demand in needing of architect is still high. The thing that I struggling the most is time management and find a new concept. We can't take the idea that we like and others don't. It must be suitable for the client, set to the landscape and environment.

Let step into the process of working in a project. First, you have to find a concept that suit to the land and environment and a quick hand sketching. Then go for concept development, and the last stage is the technical drawing stage. Last but not least, to make your university journey smooth in architecture, I recommend you should learn a technical skill such as Auto cad, SketchUp, Rhino, Lumion. etc. before getting into university, because it prepares you for technical expertise and has more time to focus on the project. At the end I want to tell you that "If you start doing something, just keep doing, great things take time".
