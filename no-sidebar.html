Welcome

We break the rule and live the life we want!

We are a blog where everyone can seek for tips and read the experience about student. Student Phobia is founded in 2020. The intention of this webpage is to help people who get lost and don't know what to do next. We upload the content that related to education, travel, tips in technology, some inspirational quotes and share experience from the student that currently struggling with their life as a university student.

Our Mission

At Student Phobia, we want to share the tips and some inspiration article to help encourage the student. We know finding the motivation to encourage yourself is not easy. Leave everything behind and go for your dream is hard too. It is sad when you know the things will happen and you can't do any about it. YES, people have a different struggle. However, is it really worth to spend your entire life doing the things that you do not want?
